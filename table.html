WHERE GOOD TIMES AND GREAT DRINKS MEET

Come to Pub & Lounge and unwind with your favorite drink while
enjoying our vibrant decor and lively ambiance. Our talente

MEET THE HOST

BROOKLYN SIMMONS

As a Pub & Lounge host, you are the first point of contact for our
guests. Your warm and welcoming personality sets the tone for
their entire experience with us. You are responsible for providing
excellent customer service, making sure that every guest feels
valued and appreciated.

In addition to greeting and seating guests, you will also take their
orders and ensure that they are served promptly and correctly.
You will be knowledgeable about our menu offerings and be able
to answer any questions that guests may have.
